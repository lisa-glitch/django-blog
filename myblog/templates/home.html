About Relationship Haven

Relationship Haven is a Nairobi-based community organization.
We champion for the rights of women and children through activities such as:

  • Youth and Women Mentorship and Empowerment
  • Child Rescue
  • Environmentalism

Services

We envision a brighter world for humanity.
Therefore, we aim to touch one life at a time, with the hope that the beneficiaries will one day transform other lives

Our mission is to bridge the gaps in humanity through our services.
Our vision is to be the best humanitarian organization in East Africa in the next 10 years.
Our organization has three leaders who work hand-in hand with the community and ensures that our impact is felt at grassroot level.
So far, our funding comes from well-wishers. We are calling on anyone who is willing to donate or work with us to reach out.

Our Leaders

The three co-founders all bring to the table unmatched experience and expertise.
Before coming together, each of them was already carrying out acts of service and love within the community.
